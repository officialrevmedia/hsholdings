About H&S Holdings

A diversified group built on operational depth and disciplined execution.

Established in 2015, H&S Holdings is a North American group of companies serving commercial, residential, institutional, hospitality, healthcare, and industrial environments.

Who We Are

Built around the needs of complex environments

H&S Holdings is a group of specialized companies operating across construction, building maintenance, interior design, hygiene solutions, and hospitality support services. Each company within the group brings dedicated leadership and a focused service mandate. Together, they share infrastructure, operating standards, and a long-term commitment to client outcomes.

The group was established in 2015 and has grown into a multi-brand operating platform with a workforce of more than 500 across Canada and the United States. The expansion has been driven by client relationships, sector experience, and the addition of complementary service capabilities.

How We Operate

A portfolio that operates as one

We operate the group as a portfolio. Each company maintains its own brand, leadership, and client relationships, while shared support functions ensure clients receive consistent service regardless of which company is engaged.

01

Integrated capability

Clients can engage one company or several, with coordination handled at the group level. Engagement scope can expand without changing the relationship.

02

Cross-sector experience

The group serves hospitality, government, healthcare, industrial, commercial, residential, and pharmaceutical clients. Experience across sectors shapes how we approach every engagement.

03

Long-tenure relationships

Most of the group’s business is recurring, with relationships measured in years rather than projects.
