About

Premium hydration, made responsibly in Canada

Waterr is the premium water program within the H&S Group. Designed for luxury hospitality, Waterr replaces single-use plastic with elegant glass and aluminum packaging, purified through an eleven-step process and delivered in reusable crates.

The program is built for hotels, restaurants, and resorts that want a sustainable water solution that still looks and feels premium on the table.

The Program

What we deliver

Core elements of the Waterr program as part of the H&S Group.

  • Still and sparkling water
  • Glass and aluminum packaging
  • 11-step purification
  • Delivery in reusable crates
  • Made in Canada
  • Single-use plastic reduction
Sustainability

Elegance without the environmental cost.

Every element of Waterr is designed to reduce single-use plastic while elevating the guest experience. Reusable crates, recyclable packaging, and a Canadian supply chain keep the program both responsible and dependable.

Backed by the operational discipline of the H&S Group, Waterr delivers consistent supply and account management across multi-property hospitality clients.
