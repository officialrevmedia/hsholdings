About

Project work backed by skilled trades and experienced site management

HS Construction is the construction division of H&S Holdings. The company delivers new builds, renovations, and project work for clients who require durable outcomes, efficient execution, and a reliable single point of accountability.

Project work is supported by skilled trades, experienced supervision, and disciplined coordination between sub-trades. The team is built to operate alongside active business operations when required.

Capabilities

What we deliver

Core services delivered by HS Construction as part of the H&S group.

  • New construction
  • Renovations and retrofits
  • Tenant improvements
  • Project management
  • Site supervision and trades coordination
  • Pre-construction planning
Approach

Structured planning. Transparent communication.

HS Construction operates with structured pre-construction planning, transparent communication, and disciplined site management. Projects are scoped with the client’s operating environment in mind, particularly for clients with occupied facilities and active operations.

Each project receives a defined point of accountability, structured reporting, and the operational discipline of the wider H&S group.
