About

Integrated service. Single-supplier convenience.

Purus is a national building maintenance company within the H&S group, serving clients across hospitality, government, pharmaceutical, and commercial environments. The company is built around integrated service delivery, single-supplier convenience, and consistent communication standards.

Client engagements are managed by dedicated account leads with operational oversight from senior management. Reporting is structured. Communication is predictable. Response times are defined.

Capabilities

What we deliver

Core services delivered by Purus as part of the H&S group.

  • Integrated facility maintenance
  • Janitorial services
  • Recurring building care
  • 24/7 operational coverage
  • Account management and reporting
  • Multi-site service programs
Approach

Defined service standards. Transparent communication.

Purus operates with defined service standards, transparent communication, and the integrity required to serve clients in regulated and high-traffic environments. Engagements are designed for tenure, with structured account reviews and continuous improvement built into every program.

For clients with multi-site portfolios, Purus operates with regional account leadership and centralized coordination.
