Careers at H&S

Build your future with H&S.

H&S Holdings is built by people who take pride in service, execution, and excellence. Across our group of companies, we offer opportunities for individuals who want to grow within construction, maintenance, design, hospitality, hygiene solutions, operations, and client service.

Why Work Here

A place to do real operational work

The H&S group offers careers across six operating companies, with real responsibility, real client work, and a path for growth. Our people are operators first. We hire for craft, attitude, and follow-through, and we promote based on demonstrated execution.

Whether you join a construction site team, a maintenance crew, a hospitality program, an interior design studio, or a corporate function, you become part of a group that values consistency and continuous improvement.

Where We Hire

Roles across the group

The H&S group is actively building teams across the following functional areas. Specific openings change throughout the year.

01

Skilled Trades and Construction

Carpenters, finishers, project coordinators, site supervisors, and trades professionals working with HS Construction.

02

Building Maintenance and Facility Services

Facility technicians, cleaning professionals, supervisors, and operations leaders working with Purus and BlueSky.

03

Interior Design and Staging

Design assistants, project designers, and staging coordinators working with Align Interiors.

04

Hospitality Services

Hospitality service professionals, supervisors, and program coordinators working with Hash Hospitality.

05

Hygiene Solutions and Supply

Account managers, logistics coordinators, and customer service professionals working with Stella Hygiene.

06

Corporate Functions

Sales, marketing, finance, human resources, business development, and administrative roles supporting the group.

How We Hire

A straightforward process

Our process is straightforward. After a written application and initial conversation, candidates speak with the hiring leader directly. We make decisions promptly and communicate transparently throughout.

We hire for the work and for the team. References, demonstrated skill, and operating attitude matter more than credentials alone.
