Sector Experience

Trusted across diverse sectors.

The H&S group delivers services across a broad cross section of commercial, institutional, and consumer-facing environments. Sector experience is what allows the group to mobilize quickly and deliver predictable outcomes across complex sites.

Hotels, resorts, and hospitality groups across Canada and the United States

Hospitality

The H&S group is deeply experienced in hospitality, with more than 30 hotel and resort clients and more than two decades of operational experience inside hotel environments. Services include building maintenance, hygiene products, hospitality operations support, design, and construction.

Federal, provincial, state, and municipal facilities

Government

The group serves federal, provincial, state, and municipal facilities with maintenance and hygiene programs designed for high-traffic, security-sensitive, and compliance-driven environments.

Hospitals, clinics, and healthcare administration sites

Healthcare

Healthcare environments require uncompromising hygiene, predictable service, and consistent execution. The group delivers maintenance and hygiene programs to hospitals, clinics, and healthcare administration sites.

Manufacturing, logistics, and industrial operations

Industrial

Industrial clients receive maintenance programs tailored to manufacturing, logistics, and operational environments where safety and uptime are paramount.

Office portfolios, corporate campuses, commercial real estate

Commercial

Commercial clients include corporate office portfolios, commercial real estate operators, and multi-tenant property managers. Services span construction, renovation, design, maintenance, and hygiene supply.

Multi-family residential and high-end private residences

Residential

Residential clients are served through HS Construction and Align Interiors, with experience across multi-family residential developments and high-end private residences.

Retail environments and customer-facing commercial spaces

Retail

Retail clients receive maintenance, hygiene, and renovation programs designed around customer-facing environments and operating hours.

Regulated pharmaceutical environments and laboratories

Pharmaceutical

Pharmaceutical clients receive maintenance and hygiene programs aligned with regulated operating environments. Service standards reflect the compliance requirements of pharmaceutical operations.

National and regional property management firms

Property Management

Property management firms and multi-site portfolio operators benefit from the group’s coordinated approach, with single-point accountability across building maintenance, hygiene supply, and renovation services.

Premium resort and hotel operators across North America

Resorts and Hotels

Premium resort and hotel operators receive integrated service programs from across the group, supported by dedicated hospitality experience through Hash Hospitality and operational discipline through Purus and BlueSky.
